## What is Pomo?

Pomo is a terminal-based Pomodoro timer built with [Bubble Tea](https://github.com/charmbracelet/bubbletea) that brings focus and productivity tracking directly to your command line. Whether you're coding, writing, or working on any task that requires deep focus, Pomo helps you maintain productive work sessions with built-in break reminders.

## Statistics and tracking

Track your productivity over time with the `pomo stats` command:

![Stats](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Bahaaio/pomo/main/.github/assets/stats.png)

- **Duration ratio** - Compare total work time vs break time
- **Weekly bar chart** - Visualize daily work hours for the past 7 days
- **4-month heatmap** - GitHub-style activity visualization showing your work patterns

<Warning>
  The heatmap visualization requires a [Nerd Font](https://www.nerdfonts.com/) to display correctly.
</Warning>
